Rules

Online Duel

Each room seats two players. The server owns the deck, hands, turns, token use, shots, stuns, sweeps, locks, and win checks.

Basics

  • At the start of your turn, draw until you have 6 cards if your deck has enough cards.
  • You must place exactly 1 card before ending your turn.
  • You may use up to 2 tokens on your turn.
  • Cards can be placed on empty tiles or over lower-numbered cards.
  • 3 cannot be covered, swept, or shot unless Armor Pierce is used for the shot.
  • Stunned tiles cannot be played on or shot.
  • Four top cards in a row wins.

Player Wins!
